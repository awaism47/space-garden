Space GARD3N is an instagram filter that allows you to know in real time the ideal time and place to plant your favorite fruit or vegetables. Using AR technology, Space GARD3N compares data from NASA satellites and studies on the needs of the biosphere to let you know easily and easily where and when it may be ideal for your agricultural project.This project is developed within the framework of SpaceAppsChallenge 2020 with a multidisciplinary team with people from different countries and nationalities united to combat the food shortage caused by the crisis generated by COVI-19 that left thousands of people without resources throughout the world
